Specialized template and framework for building a personal knowledge management (PKM) system in Obsidian, optimized for interaction with AI agents. It organizes information into categories (Second Brain for ephemeral and chronological data, Wiki for formal knowledge) and supports automated ingestion and knowledge synthesis.
